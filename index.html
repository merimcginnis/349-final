a little bit about me!

I’m Meri, a Web Developer specializing in Front-End Development and User Experience.

Currently, I’m studying Information Science at MSU, where I’m exploring how data, design, and tech intersect to create meaningful digital experiences. But my journey started a bit differently— I graduated from Lansing Community College with an Associate of Art Foundations, so I’ve always had one foot in the world of creativity and the other in the realm of technology. Why not merge them? I believe tech isn’t just about code, it’s about making the world a more expressive, innovative place. That’s why I’m on a mission to bridge the gap between art and tech—whether I’m coding, designing, or dreaming up new projects. Looking ahead, I’m gearing up for grad school at either Boston University or Loyola Chicago, where I’ll be diving deep into Software Engineering. I’m excited to sharpen my skills and expand my horizons, because, for me, the intersection of art and tech is where the real magic happens. When I’m not studying or coding, you’ll probably find me sketching, creating digital art, or just contemplating the next big idea that combines my love for both the creative and technical sides of the world.
